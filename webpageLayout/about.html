About

Cards was created by Team Platypus (Ian Glazko, Paige Hanson, Andrw Lathrop, David Morehouse, Marco Versellie) for Michigan Techonological University's Team Software Project class.
Cards is meant to be a multiplayer online card game website where people can play card games against other people.
