Rules

Texas Hold 'Em

Each player is dealt two cards. Each player looks at their cards a can choose to bet or fold. After the betting has taken place, the dealer lays three cards face up in the center. Then there is a second round of betting where the players can either bet or fold. After the betting takes place, the dealer lays a fourth card face up in the center. A third round of betting takes places where players can either bet or fold. After the betting takes place, the dealer lays a fifth card face up in the center. The final round of betting takes place. The player with the best hand wins. If all of the players expect for one fold, the player who did not fold automatically wins. This game uses standard poker hands. There is a maximum of four players.

War

War is a two player game. Each player is initially given half of the deck. The players simultaneously flip over the top card from their deck. The player with the highest card wins and gets to keep all of the cards in the center. If both cards are the same value, those cards remain in the center, and each player flips over another card. The player who runs out of cards first loses.
